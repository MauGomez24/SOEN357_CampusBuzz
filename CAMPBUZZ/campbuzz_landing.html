About CampusBuzz

CampusBuzz bridges the communication gap between Concordia University students and their associations. By offering a centralized digital noticeboard, students can stay informed about extracurricular activities, events, and opportunities tailored to their interests.

Whether you're looking for academic workshops, social events, or career-building opportunities, CampusBuzz makes it easy to discover and participate in activities across campus.

Features Designed for Students

Discover Events

Explore campus events tailored to your interests with advanced filters and recommendations.

Real-Time Updates

Stay informed with notifications from your favorite associations.

Connect & Engage

Interact with peers, RSVP to events, and grow your network within the campus community.

How It Works

  1. Sign up with your Concordia email to create a personalized profile.
  2. Follow your favorite associations and set your interests.
  3. Browse events, RSVP, and sync them with your calendar.
  4. Stay connected with real-time notifications and updates.

For Student Associations

CampusBuzz isn't just for students—it’s also a powerful tool for associations. Promote your events, increase participation, and manage RSVPs effortlessly through our intuitive interface. With CampusBuzz, your reach is campus-wide.
